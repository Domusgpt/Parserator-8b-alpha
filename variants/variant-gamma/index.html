Architect → Extractor lifecycle

Parserator orchestrates every parse with layered safeguards.

  1. Architect

    Gemini 1.5 Flash drafts a SearchPlan tuned to your schema and domain.

  2. Extractor

    Deterministic heuristics walk the plan, invoking the LLM only on low-confidence paths.

  3. Assurance

    Parserator surfaces confidence, token, and fallback telemetry with every JSON payload.
